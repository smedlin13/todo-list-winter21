Beta Medlin

Zach and I found Beta on KSL and adopted her when she was 6 months old. She's a border-point (a mix between a border collie and german short-haired pointer). We take her running, hiking, and mountain biking. It takes a lot of exercise to make her tired. We love snuggling with her after a full-day of activities!
